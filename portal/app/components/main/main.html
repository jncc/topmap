GI Mapping Portal

This service is in development it should only be used by members of the development team and user group


This service is a light-weight tool through which to quickly explore spatial data offered through an OGC compliant web service primarily through its WMS (Web Mapping Service). It was developed by the Joint Nature Conservation Committee as part of the drive to offer an easy way to access and manage our spatial data holdings. This portal has been created to manage Earth Observation Datasets and deliver them to interested parties either via OGC web-services (WMS, WFS, WCS, etc...) or via direct download where possible.

This service is still under development and feedback is appreciated while we develop this service.
