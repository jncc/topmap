Earth Observation Centre of Excellence Pilot Collaboration Node

This portal has been created to share earth observation data and other widely used geographic information (GI) datasets across the DEFRA Group. This is to save time and money by managing and delivering these large datasets centrally, rather than by each individual member.

The portal is in the very early stages of development and we need your help to make it fit for your purpose. As you use this portal, please send us your comments on how to improve the service you receive. We welcome your comments on any aspect of the system: functionality, content, interface and layout, and we will feed these into the development and release cycle (see Roadmap).

The data contained within this portal can be accessed through this online interface, through the API, or through OGC services (data streaming and download to local servers, GI software, and other data nodes or portals).
